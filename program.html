Accepted presentations

We are happy to announce that Kim Hamilton Duffy (MIT) and Hugo Krawczyk (Algorand Foundation) accepted our invitations to give keynote presentations. In addition, there will be six contributed talks:
  • On Account Recovery and Delegation in WebAuthn by Nick Frymann (Surrey Centre for Cyber Security, University of Surrey, UK), Daniel Gardham (Information Security Group, Royal Holloway, University of London, UK), Mark Manulis (Surrey Centre for Cyber Security, University of Surrey, UK)

  • Dynamic Universal Accumulator with Batch Update over Bilinear Groups by Giuseppe Vitto, Alex Biryukov (all authors from DCS&SnT, University of Luxembourg, Luxembourg)

  • Breaking and Fixing Contact Identifier-based Mutual Authentication in Apple AirDrop by Alexander Heinrich, Matthias Hollick, Thomas Schneider, Milan Stute, Christian Weinert (all authors from Technical University of Darmstadt, Germany)

  • DID Recovery via a TimeStamping Service by Omer Shlomovits (ZenGo-X), Hicham Batou (Planetworks), Jakub Jastrzębski (Planetworks)

  • The regulatory framework of digital identification services across the European Union by Cristina Timón López, Ignacio Alamillo Domingo, Julián Valero Torrijos (all authors from University of Murcia, Spain)

  • Delegatable Anonymous Credentials from Mercurial Signatures by Elizabeth Crites, Anna Lysyanskaya (all authors from Brown University, Providence, US)
