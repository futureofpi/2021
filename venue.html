Location

The Future of PI workshop will be held in co-location with EuroS&P 2021 and take place in Vienna, Austria virtually on 2021, Sep 6. See also the EuroS&P 2021 workshop list.

Sponsors

Future of PI receives support from the Horizon 2020 project Olympus.

COVID19 notice

It is noticed that at the current time it remains unclear whether the conference can take place as planned as a physical event, or will have to be converted to a virtual or semi-virtual event. In the latter case, the workshop chairs will follow the decision made for the hosting conference and organize the Future of PI workshop as an all- or semi-virtual event as well.

Like EuroS&P 2021, the Future of PI workshop will be held virtually.
