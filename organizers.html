Workshop organizers

Julia Hesse (IBM Research – Zurich)

Julia is a Postdoctoral Researcher at IBM Research – Zurich. Her research focuses on provable security, and she is particularly interested in password-based cryptography and privacy-preserving protocols.
Anja Lehmann (HPI & University of Potsdam)

Anja is a full professor at the Hasso-Plattner-Institute, University of Potsdam where she leads the Cybersecurity and Identity Management group. Anja works on the development and analysis of cryptographic protocols that can guarantee the security of modern applications in a provably-secure manner. Current focus areas are secure and user-friendly identity management, password-based protocols, as well as privacy-preserving technologies.
Bertram Poettering (IBM Research – Zurich)

Bertram is a Research Staff Member at IBM Research – Zurich. After obtaining his Ph.D. on privacy-preserving authentication, today his research covers all areas of every-day cryptography, ranging from protocol design to analysis and from theoretic contributions to implementations.
